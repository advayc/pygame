Safety Enhancements and Challenges:
Self-driving cars aim to significantly improve road safety by eliminating human error.
EV safety requires comparing their non-collision rates with human drivers under various conditions.
EVs perform well in tasks like lane-keeping and maintaining safe distances.
Cost and Convenience:
High costs of sensors and computing components are a barrier to widespread EV adoption.
Consumers are willing to pay for autonomous functions due to benefits such as increased productivity.
EVs could transform mobility by allowing passengers to use travel time for leisure or work.
By 2030, shared autonomous EV's could become the most cost-effective and convenient transportation.
