How to play?
1. Move character with WASD or arrow keys!
2. You're given a roadmap with the essential parts of electric cars!
3. You're sent to a track and your goal is to go to EV Stations!
4. Each EV station provides information about self driving cars.
5. When touching the EV stations you will be sent to a lesson sector
6. Click the next button to go to the activity! Submit your answers!
7. If you are wrong you will have to answer another question until right.
8. Make sure to not hit the barriers ... your car is already broken!
9. Continue this process to collect every part!! GOOD LUCK!
