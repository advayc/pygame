Self driving cars use many different technologies in combination to be able to sense and react to their surroundings. 
The types of hardware that self driving cars use include cameras to be able to see everything.
Radar to detect speed of objects lasers to create a 3d map of your surroundings and ultrasonic sensors.
Self driving cars also use many different types of software. 
GPS is a very important software that they use, allowing the cars to navigate from one location to another. 
Self driving cars use algorithms to be able to recognize foreign objects such as people on the road and road signs.
